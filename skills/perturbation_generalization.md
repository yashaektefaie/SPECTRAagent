# Perturbation Generalization Skill

Use this skill for single-cell perturbation, drug response, and genetic
perturbation benchmarks.

Common spectral properties:

- Perturbation target pathway or ontology distance.
- Gene network distance.
- Drug embedding or mechanism distance.
- Cell-type or cell-state distance.
- Batch, study, or assay context.
- Systematic shift magnitude.

Required controls:

- Separate perturbation-specific effects from systematic variation.
- Check unseen perturbation and unseen covariate settings independently.
- Report confounders that are not captured by the chosen spectral property.
